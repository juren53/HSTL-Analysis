
Here's my best attempt at explaining the complexities of sound metadata.

1. the NARA catalog (for sound published as of a couple of years ago with potentially wrong or duplicate records)

2. the Drupal website (for sound that is currently published, as well as unpublished). This data is more accurate for the published sound.

I am attaching examples exports of each here. I deleted a lot of the columns that are unnecessary.

Priority 1. I would like to compare each export spreadsheet using the NAID columns in both.

- Where there are NAIDS in the NARA catalog but not in Drupal, I would like those records to appear in a separate spreadsheet tab.
